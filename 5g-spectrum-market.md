<p><p>The 5G spectrum market consists of various frequency bands, primarily C-Band and Millimeter Wave (mmWave). </p><p>C-Band operates within 3.7 to 4.2 GHz, providing a balance between coverage and capacity, making it ideal for urban areas. </p><p>In contrast, mmWave operates above 24 GHz, offering extremely high data rates and low latency but with limited coverage and penetration, making it suitable for dense environments like stadiums and city centers. Both bands are crucial for 5G deployment, catering to different connectivity needs.</p></p>
