<p><p>Methylene Diphenyl Isocyanate (MDI) is a key chemical used primarily in the production of rigid polyurethane foams, cases, and adhesives. The MDI market can be segmented into two main types: Pure MDI and Polymeric MDI. Pure MDI is characterized by its high purity and is typically used in applications requiring precise formulations, such as coatings and elastomers. Polymeric MDI, on the other hand, consists of a mixture of MDI oligomers, often utilized in manufacturing flexible foams and insulation materials due to its cost-effectiveness and versatility.</p></p>
