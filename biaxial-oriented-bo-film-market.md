<p><p>Biaxial Oriented (BO) films are categorized into several types based on their material properties and applications. Biaxial Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) films are known for their excellent clarity and moisture resistance, making them ideal for packaging. Biaxial Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate (BoPET) films offer superior strength and thermal stability, commonly used in electronics and graphics. Biaxial Oriented Polyamide (BOPA) films combine strong barrier properties with flexibility, making them suitable for food packaging and other applications requiring durability. Each type serves specific industry needs.</p></p>
