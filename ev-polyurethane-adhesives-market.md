<p><p>The EV polyurethane adhesives market encompasses two primary types: water-based and solvent-based polyurethane adhesives. Water-based polyurethane adhesives are environmentally friendly, offering low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions and are suitable for various substrates, making them ideal for applications in automotive and construction. In contrast, solvent-based polyurethane adhesives provide strong bonding capabilities and are often used in industrial applications, but they contain higher VOC levels, posing environmental and health concerns. Both types serve specific market needs based on their performance characteristics.</p></p>
