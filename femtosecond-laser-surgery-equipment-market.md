<p><p>The femtosecond laser surgery equipment market is categorized into full femtosecond and half femtosecond types based on their pulse duration and application capabilities. Full femtosecond lasers emit pulses in the range of a few hundred femtoseconds, providing precise cutting and minimal thermal damage, ideal for complex procedures like cataract and refractive surgery. Half femtosecond lasers, with slightly longer pulse durations, offer a balance between speed and precision, making them suitable for less intricate surgeries while still ensuring reduced recovery times and improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
