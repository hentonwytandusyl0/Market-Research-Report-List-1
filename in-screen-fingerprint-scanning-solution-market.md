<p><p>In-screen fingerprint scanning solutions primarily include two types: optical and ultrasonic. Optical scanners capture fingerprint images using light, providing a straightforward and cost-effective solution for devices. They tend to be thinner and can operate under various conditions. In contrast, ultrasonic scanners utilize sound waves to create a 3D image of the fingerprint, offering enhanced security and performance, especially in challenging environments. Both types cater to a growing demand for biometric authentication in smartphones and other devices.</p></p>
