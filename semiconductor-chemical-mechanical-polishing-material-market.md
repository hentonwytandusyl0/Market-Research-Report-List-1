<p><p>The Semiconductor Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) material market comprises two primary types: CMP slurries and CMP pads. CMP slurries are liquid mixtures containing abrasive particles and chemical agents that facilitate the polishing of semiconductor wafers by removing material effectively and uniformly. CMP pads, on the other hand, are porous pads made from advanced materials that provide the surface for wafer polishing. Together, these components play a crucial role in achieving the desired surface finish and flatness essential for semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
