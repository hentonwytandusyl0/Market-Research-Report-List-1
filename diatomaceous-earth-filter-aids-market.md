<p><p>Diatomaceous earth filter aids are utilized in various filtration applications, classified primarily into three market types: Filter Pink (FP) Grades, Filter White (FW) Grades, and Filter Natural (FN) Grades. FP Grades consist of calcined diatomaceous earth, offering high filtration efficiency and durability. FW Grades, characterized by their high purity and whiteness, are suitable for food and beverage filtration. FN Grades, derived from natural sources, provide a more economical option for general-purpose filtration, balancing performance and cost-effectiveness across different industries.</p></p>
