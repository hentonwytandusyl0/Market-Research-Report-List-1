<p><p>The Ultrasonic Show-B Diagnostic Equipment market encompasses two primary types: Continuous Wave Ultrasound Equipment and Pulsed Wave Ultrasound Equipment. Continuous Wave Ultrasound uses a steady stream of sound waves to produce real-time imaging, ideal for monitoring blood flow and heart conditions. In contrast, Pulsed Wave Ultrasound sends short bursts of sound waves, enabling precise depth measurements and imaging for various diagnostic applications. Each type serves distinct medical purposes, contributing to enhanced patient care and diagnostics in healthcare settings.</p></p>
