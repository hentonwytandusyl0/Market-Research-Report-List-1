<p><p>Cloud-based business analytics software is categorized into three market types: Private Cloud, Public Cloud, and Hybrid Cloud. Private Cloud offers dedicated resources, providing enhanced security and customization for businesses with strict data governance requirements. Public Cloud, on the other hand, delivers scalability and cost-effectiveness by hosting services on shared infrastructure, making it accessible to a wide range of users. Hybrid Cloud combines both models, allowing organizations to leverage public clouds for flexibility while maintaining sensitive operations on private clouds for added control and security.</p></p>
