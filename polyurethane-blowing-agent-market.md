<p><p>The polyurethane blowing agent market is primarily segmented into tube and gun-style applications. Tube-style systems involve pre-measured, ready-to-use formulations that are ideal for producing foam in specific molds. They offer convenience and precise control over the blowing agent dosage. In contrast, gun-style applications utilize portable dispensing equipment, allowing for on-site mixing and application of blowing agents. This method is versatile, enabling operators to adjust the foam consistency and density, catering to various insulation and construction needs effectively.</p></p>
